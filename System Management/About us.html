Mission

Heroes organization shall provide high quality items that comes from Cavite State University Imus Campus, with reasonable price and shall be delivered with care. Ensuring the quantity and quality of the item. We are to provide good hospitality to every customers.

Vision

To be Locally know by the Caviteños and stand at the local market where everyone can see and buy the product of the Cavite State University – Imus Campus.

Objectives

In addition to, and in support of its mission and to achieve of its vision, the HEROES Org. shall:

  • Provide adequate customer service.
  • Relevant informations on the website.
  • High quality products that came on the University.
  • Acknowledge the customers request and use it for the growth of the said business.
